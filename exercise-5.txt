Exercise 5

List all of the emails associated to AddressBook.id = 100


SELECT ElectronicMail.content 
FROM AddressBook 
JOIN Entry ON AddressBook.id = Entry.addressBookId 
JOIN ElectronicMail ON Entry.id = ElectronicMail.entryId 
WHERE AddressBook.id = 100 
AND Entry.type = 'electronic-mail';
